About Crier Subscriptions

Traditional Subscriptions: Readers pay a monthly fee for full access to your content. You set the price (default $5/mo). Supporter tiers available for those who want to pay more.

Pay-Per-Read: Readers pay per individual post (default $0.10). Great for casual readers who don't want a subscription. Also supports x402 protocol for AI agent payments.

Stripe Connect: Payments go directly to your Stripe account. The platform can optionally take a small percentage (configurable). This also enables verified earnings tracking for the Fountain network.

Machine Payments (x402): AI agents can pay for content access automatically using the HTTP 402 protocol. This is the future of agent-to-agent commerce.
